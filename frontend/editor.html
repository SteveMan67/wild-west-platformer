Getting Around

You can move the view with WASD, middle mouse button click and drag, or the minimap.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • E - toggle eraser
  • 1-7 - switch categories
  • <shift><scroll> - switch blocks within a category
  • P - toggle play mode
  • O - open file
  • R - rotate block (editor)
  • R - respawn (play mode)
  • <Esc> - Close menu
  • Selection Tools
  • <Shift><drag>
  • <drag> - Move selection around
  • F - Fill Selection with currently selected block
  • R - Rotate all rotatable blocks within the selection 90°
  • E - Erase whole selection

Triggers

Triggers can be found in the sixth category with a toggle icon. Once you place a trigger, right click it to edit what it will do. Triggers will execute their steps from top to bottom.

Trigger Functions:

  • Swap red and blue - swaps the red and blue blocks
  • Teleport - teleports the player to a given x/y, can be instant or have camera animation
  • Rotate - rotates a block at a specific x/y position a given amount of degrees
  • Change Block - Changes a block at a specific x/y to the given block
  • Delay - stops execution for a given amount of miliseconds
